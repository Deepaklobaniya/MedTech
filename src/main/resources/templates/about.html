AboutUs

Medtech will completely eliminate the mental turmoil you were forced to endure in treatment of your relative. In fact, you won’t have to went to hospital for any detail of your patient admitted in the hospital.

Everyone require treatment and health information in day to day life, whether it is out for small injuries, normal treatment or for saving life. Despite the recent trend in online appointment for treatment, there will always be a need for time to time update of a patient for his family and relative.

A lot of time is required for details of patient medical report, medicines and transactions or test reports. Today’s society desires efficient and easy methods to complete their tasks. Thus, there is a need for solutions to alleviate person stress by reducing time spent on meet when their relatives are admitted in hospital.

Live Update

The idea is to help people from different fields, places and backgrounds to have a platform where they can easily get the live update of patient admitted in the hospital without much trouble since the web application will be made as simple and efficient as possible.Instead of looking around for treatment, you can easily get appointment, details and qualifications of doctors, medical test, reports and medicines and live update of patient.

Improved Experience

Checking out is instant-you don't waste time standing in queues. Don't forget, you save time that would otherwise be wasted in travelling to and do admission process. It's now common to see someone book online appointments for treatment, medicines and payment. But given the state of traffic in big cities and the lack of time, online patient live update seems to be the next big thing. Like any online hospital management system, patient live update system also have a larger variety of options available compared to what you find at your local hospitals.

Easy To Use

The goal of the project is to create a web application that will reduce the amount of time and stress spent for waiting to get the health information by providing them live update of their patient health in every one hour. This application fixes the limitation and problems of paper based processes. The main goal is to provide comfort and easiness by making the new technology of web pages design easier and to search all possible live details of patient.
